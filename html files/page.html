Heating Installation Costs & Long-Term Value

Heating systems can be expensive. We’re talking multiple thousands of dollars as an initial investment, and some can require financing. This is not a decision that should be made lightly and we don’t want any customers gambling with such expensive equipment by hiring an amateur or a family friend to do the job. Leaving this up to a trained professional not only alleviates any legal consequences (like hefty fines from tampering with municipal gas lines), but it keeps your home safe and keeps botched installations out of the question.

Also, if you’re a homeowner who is on a strict budget, we urge you to take a look at our financing options and some of the federal tax credits, incentives, and promotions that could help you save money. This is an amazing time for homeowners who are willing to take the leap toward newer, more energy-efficient technology. There are plenty of incentives funded from the Inflation Reduction Act that can give you thousands of dollars back on the purchase of a new furnace installation, heat pump, or ductless mini split.

Work with our team to take advantage of these incentives and promotions.

Heating Installation Timeline: What to Expect

Heating systems need to be connected to a variety of different parts of your home. They need to be hooked up to air ducts, electrical wiring, and even gas lines depending on the type of system you purchase. All of this can take hours, but with a professional, it’s much more streamlined and straightforward. So if you’re looking for a dependable heat pump installer or furnace technician, our licensed experts will make sure the job is done right the first time.

Professional heating system installation can come with estimates on how long it will take so you can plan your day accordingly. It should never really be more than 8 hours, but sometimes we can run into situations that complicate things. We will always be proactive and communicate clearly when the ending of your installation is approaching.
